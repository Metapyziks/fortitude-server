Help - Fortitude Application

These questions relate to the Fortitude Application used to play the game.

  • How do I play the game?

    Download the app and you'll get to put all your strategies made here on the website into practice.

  • How do I view details about a cache through the app?

    Click on the marker for that cache on the map. This will show you the details about an enemy cache, one of your own caches or an outlaw camp.

  • How do I navigate to a cache using the app?

    Click on the marker for that cache on the map, from here there is a button to plan route. This will put a line on the map from your position to the cache with the shortest route we can find.

  • How do I scout a cache?

    Navigate to the cache you want to scout, remember it can't be one you own.
    When you're there you'll notice the castle button at the bottom of the map is no longer greyed out and you can click on it; this will bring up the screen from which you can scout.

  • How do I attack a cache?

    Navigate to the cache you want to attack, remember it can't be one you own.
    When you're there you'll notice the castle button at the bottom of the map is no longer greyed out and you can click on it. Once you have there will be the option to attack the cache.

  • How do I manage my cache's defending armies?

    Navigate to the cache you want to manage.

    When you're there you'll notice the castle button at the bottom of the map is no longer greyed out and you can click on it. Once you have, there will be the option to withdraw and deposit soldiers from the defending army.

  • How do I place a cache?

    Make sure you are far enough away from any other cache.
    Once you are, the flag button at the bottom of the map will no longer be greyed out and you can click on it.
    When you click on the flag button, you can choose how many soldiers you want to place the cache with.
    These will become the defending army except a few which will die in the construction of the cache.

  • Why is there a blue circle on the message symbol at the bottom of the map?

    You have a notification. Click the symbol to view your notifications and from here you can click an individual notification to see more details.

  • How do I use the messaging feature of the app?

    Either view your account by clicking at the bar on top of the map. From here you click on messages.
    Or you can click on an enemy's cache on the map and send them a message from here.
    Lastly, you can click on a message from your notifications.

  • Why do I need my Wi-Fi on?

    If you have your Wi-Fi on you have the opportunity to find rare treasure areas.
    These are found if you can connect to the Wi-Fi of special Mac addresses.
    However we won't tell you which Mac address can give you treasure and when so make sure Wi-Fi is on at all times.
