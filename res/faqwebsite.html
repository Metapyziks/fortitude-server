Help - Fortitude Website

  • How do I create an account?

    You can do this through the side bar, or the main login page.
    Make sure you activate your account through the email we will send to you to access the full website.

  • How do I use the map?

    You can use the map to look for caches by location, user name or cache name by typing in the search bar and clicking the search button. You can filter the search to only your caches, enemy caches or outlaw camps with the dropbar on the top left hand side. This will automatically update so no need for you to click search again.

  • How do I update my account?

    You can do this through the account page.
    This will let you change your avatar through the website alone, and change your password, though you'll have to confirm this one through a link emailed to you.
    You can also delete your account which again you will need to confirm through an emailed link, however please allow for a short delay as one of our admins will need to double check this too.

  • How do I view more details about a cache?

    Simply click on the cache in the side bar or if you can't see it, click the link at the bottom of the sidebar to display more caches and click on the cache from there.

  • How do I enable javascript?

    Follow the instructions for your web browser on this page.

  • How do I play the game?

    Download the app and you'll get to put all your strategies made here into practice.

  • How do I send a message?

    Find the user via the search bar or through their cache, and send them a message when viewing their details.
